1.1 Describing the purpose of IT in modern business

IT in modern business is very useful with big or small businesses you can manage, process and store data. You can communicate with the employees, clients and customers of a business. IT can make a business more productive, increase performance and help you save money. You can advertise a business to get more customers or just to be more established. Using IT moire careers and jobs for a business can be available you can also use IT to get more employees.

1.2 Describing methods, skills and resources needed to complete digital projects successfully

To complete a digital project successfully you will need Skills such as time management, resource management, project communication, planning and organization. You will need to have good team skills, communication skills and be able to solve problems. You can make a schedule have a budget and you may need a leader or a manager who can stay on top of things and keep track of everything. you could also have someone else who hasn't worked on the project to take a look at your project so that they could give you some feedback to use and improve your project.

1.3 Plan and carry out tasks using IT – e.g. making your website. How will you plan it? Include a link to your Trello board.

I will use a Trello board to plan out making my website which uses the kanban method. I will create tasks and put everything i need to do in a list so when I am doing the task I will move it into doing so that when it is completed i can put the task into done so that i know that it is completed. I have also added an extra list called review, so when i have completed the task I can have someone look at the task and give me feedback so that I know I have completed the task correctly or if i need to add some more or even change it completely to meet the criteria.

1.4 Describe the risks that might impact digital projects – what causes digital projects to fail? Why?

A digital project can fail or be impacted by requirements of a digital project that can be unrealistic or you may not have the right tools or resources for the project and can even be because of poor planning and organization. The lack of communication between a team,bad or no organization, poor budget management could lead to failure. If there is a team working on a digital project together and I there is no management or leadership the project could be impacted because no one is overlooking the project and making sure tasks are completed in time.
